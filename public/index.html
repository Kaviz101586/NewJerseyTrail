The New Jersey Trail


You are about to embark on an epic journey through the state of New Jersey!

It has been over a year since you have been planning to attend your friends wedding. As the sun rises on the big day, your alarm remains silent. Hours later you awake to find that you have overselpt and are now in a race against the clock to arrive on time to the ceremony down at the bottom of the state in Cape May.
Your Journey Begins in Ramsey NJ, with a full tank of gas and an empty stomach. Before you can begin your trip you first must buy some supplies for the long trip ahead of you.
Good Luck!
